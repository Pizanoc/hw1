What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, programs, and data from digital attacks. It plays an important role in keeping personal information, business systems, and online services safe.

Why It Matters

As technology becomes part of everyday life, cyber threats continue to grow. Cybersecurity helps prevent data breaches, identity theft, and financial loss while protecting privacy and trust online.
